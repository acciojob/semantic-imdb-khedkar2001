Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a professional thief who specializes in infiltrating the subconscious of his targets. He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased in exchange for planting an idea into a target’s mind—a process known as inception. As the team delves deeper into layered dreams, the line between reality and imagination begins to blur.
